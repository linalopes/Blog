I work with radical imagination in science. I put in this short sentence all my dreams and experience working with electronic prototyping, wearables, IoT, creative technology, art and design for the last 10 years. In 2016, I had a chance to join a collaborative environment with artists and scientists to develop a conductive ink, a temporary tattoo that you can touch and turn on the LEDs. Since then, I've been pursuing the bio-conductive materials, bio-degradable sensors.

Imagine one day your phone run out the battery, but instead of throwing this away, you can just put this on earth with your plants or just eat it! I believe the science and technology deserve to be reimagined. And I believe we can do this in a practical way, by prototyping!
